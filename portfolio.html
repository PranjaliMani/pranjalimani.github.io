BRAND ESTABLISHMENT

Stephie's Choice goal is to target a wider market. In order to do so, the main concept is to emphasize the “new-ness” of brand into their target audience and more importantly disassociate it with image of “for people who need medical attention”. Different range of mocktails gives different appeal to the brand and stand out well in compititive market.
